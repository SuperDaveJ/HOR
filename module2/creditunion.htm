Congressional Federal Credit Union

Since 1953, the Congressional Federal Credit Union has been offering House employees such services as:

  • Full-service branches (Ford, Rayburn and Longworth HOBs) offering traveler’s checks, cashier’s checks and money orders
  • 24-hour, full-service ATMs at all House locations (Ford, Rayburn, and Longworth HOBs, and the Capitol)
  • All House branches operate during the following business hours: Monday through Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

U.S. House of Representatives Locations

Capitol Branch:       The U.S. Capitol, 25 Independence Avenue SE

Ford Branch:           Ford HOB (was House Annex II), 441 2nd St. SW

Longworth Branch:  Longworth HOB15, Independence Avenue SW

Rayburn Branch:     Rayburn HOB50, Independence Avenue SW

If you would like information on how to join the Congressional Federal Credit Union, please visit their Web site, http://www.congressionalfcu.org/index.php/, or contact a Credit Union representative by calling 202-226-3100.
