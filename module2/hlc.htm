Professional Development

House Learning Center

The House Learning Center offers a full spectrum of Information Technology, financial management, and employee development classroom training courses to help you enhance your technical and professional skills and advance your career. Training courses available to House employees are listed on one convenient monthly CAO Course Schedule that is available on HouseNet and distributed to each House office. Among the courses offered are:

  • Active Directory
  • Adobe Acrobat
  • BlackBerry Tips and Tricks
  • Congressional Accounting
  • Document Direct
  • Excel
  • Financial Administrators Seminar
  • HTML
  • Outlook
  • PowerPoint
  • Legislative Research
  • Systems Administration
  • Effective Business Writing
  • Writing Outstanding Constituent Mail
  • Stress Management
  • Smoking Cessation
  • Communications
  • Conflict Management
  • Retirement Planning

In addition to the courses provided by the House, the Learning Center also offers several courses through contractual arrangements with outside vendors. Specifically, House Intern Orientation and Staff Assistant Orientation courses are provided on a regular basis. These are listed in the monthly CAO Course Schedule.

In addition to classroom training, the House Learning Center offers over 90 online, Web-based training classes covering a broad spectrum of Information Technology, business, and professional development courses that are available to both Capitol Hill and District employees. All of these courses can be taken at any time, and they do not require that you complete the entire course. You can jump to specific topics that suit your needs and complete only those sections of the training course that interest you. In addition, a Multimedia Library is available from which to request CDs and videos of other training programs.

Registration is required for all classroom training. Please call 202-225-6002, and choose Option 3 to register. Additional information on the current course offerings, the Multimedia Library, and other Quick Reference tools is available on HouseNet, under “Training.
