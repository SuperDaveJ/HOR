Transit Benefits

The Transit Benefit Program is a program that subsidizes transit fare to ride the Metro transportation lines. House employees who currently participate in, or are eligible to participate in, the base transit benefit program will be eligible to receive House subsidized benefits up to $110 per month.

How to Participate

To participate, download the Transit Benefit Request Form—found by going to “Personnel,” and then “Transportation,” on HouseNet—and return it to your employing office. Fax the form to the Department of Transportation at 202-493-2436.Your transit benefit passes (Metrocheks) will be sent to your office.

The deadline for submitting a Transit Benefit Request Form is the first day of each month. If the first day of the month falls on a Saturday or Sunday, the deadline for submission is the last business day of the preceding month.

Please be aware that Transit Benefit Program participants cannot simultaneously maintain a House-issued parking permit.

Metro maps and Metrochek brochures are available in First Call+, B-227 Longworth HOB. You can also access an online Metro map at http://www.wmata.com/metrorail/systemmap.cfm.

If you have questions regarding the Transit Benefit Program, contact CAO Employee Services at 202-225-1435.
