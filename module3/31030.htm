New Employee Timeline
During Your First 30-60 Days

Complete benefits enrollments


 

Complete your benefits enrollments:

  •  Optional life insurance (must be completed within 31 days of hire date)
  • Health insurance coverage selection (must be completed within 60 days of hire date)
  • Vision and dental insurance coverage selection (must be completed within 60 days of hire date)
  • Federal Flexible Spending Account (FedFlex) (must be completed within 60 days of hire date)

Complete mandatory ethics training

 

 

 

 

 

 

Complete your mandatory ethics training:

  • All new officers and employees must receive ethics training within 60 days after beginning their service to the House
  • All House employees must take at least one hour of general ethics training annually
  • All officers and certain senior-level staff must take an additional hour of detailed ethics training annually
  • District Office training may be done through video replays of live training sessions held in Washington, DC. Web-based training will be developed and offered consistent with the Committee on Standards of Official Conduct's guidelines
  • Complete your ethics training certification form and turn the form into your office’s ethics certification officer
  • The dates and times of training sessions are posted on the Web Site of the Committee on Standards of Official Conduct at www.house.gov/ethics
