New Employee Timeline
On Your First Day

Complete new-hire payroll paperwork



 

 

 

 

Complete all of your new-hire payroll paperwork, including:

  • Oath of Office
  • Employment Eligibility and Verification (Form I-9)
  • Tax Withholding (W-4)
  • Certificate of Relationship/Non-Relationship
  • Election of Retirement Coverage Form (only for employees covered under CSRS or CSRS Offset)
  • Direct Deposit form, to ensure that your first paycheck is electronically deposited
  • Your office’s payroll point of contact can access the Payroll Authorization “Smart Form” via HouseNet:  https://payrollauth.house.gov/payrollauth.aspx

Obtain an ID badge (if required)

 

 

 

Complete the ID process, including:

  • Filling out the paperwork
  • Getting fingerprinted (if required)
  • Getting photographed
  • Picking up your ID badge

Review emergency preparedness information



 

Review with your supervisor the emergency procedures, for your office:

  • Learn your evacuation route
  • Learn the location of your office escape hoods
  • Provide your office with home and emergency contact information
  • Provide information for the I AM SAFE accountability program

Review keys and building access


 

Review keys and building access:

  • Obtain keys for your office suite (if required)
  • Learn different building accesses
