Welcome From House Leadership
Welcome to the U.S. House of Representatives

As you embark on your employment with the House, you are no doubt excited about joining this great institution and being part of America’s democratic process. Whether an elected Member or an employee of the House, each individual has the distinct privilege of working for the American people, maintaining the stewardship of the U.S. House of Representatives, and participating in or supporting the legislative process in “the People’s House.” Part of the legacy we all inherit as employees of the House is our responsibility to understand the rich traditions and operations of the House, many of them rooted directly in our Constitution.

In an effort to put your employment in the larger context of that tradition and to also familiarize you with the many benefits and resources available to you as an employee of the House, we are providing you with this New Employee Orientation Program. We trust your familiarity with this information will both enrich your experience as an employee and enable you to contribute to the Members and staff of “the People’s House” – and ultimately to the American people.

Thank you for visiting the orientation program. When you have completed your journey through the “House community,” be sure to complete our survey and let us know what you think.

Welcome to “The People’s House.”
