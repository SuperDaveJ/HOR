Introduction

Talk with any of your new co-workers, and it won't take long to see the pride they have in being an employee of the U.S. House of Representatives. It's a pride that comes not only from the work they do to keep our country's government moving forward, but also from being a part of such a significant and historical branch of the United States government. So let's take some time to explore the history of the House and see how it became the House of today.
