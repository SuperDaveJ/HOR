The Constitution and The House of Representatives

Article I, Section 2

Article I, Section 2 of the Constitution defines the rules under which the House operates, including:

  • House Members serve two year terms
  • House Members must be at least 25 years of age, be a citizen of the United States for at least 7 years, and must be an inhabitant of the state he/she represents
  • House seats are apportioned among the states according to population every tenth year
  • Each state is guaranteed at least one representative
