U.S. Supreme Court Building

Like the Library of Congress, the Supreme Court was also originally housed in the Capitol, occupying six different locations before its move to the current Supreme Court building in 1935. 

The Supreme Court Building includes the Supreme Court chamber and offices for the justices and their staffs.
